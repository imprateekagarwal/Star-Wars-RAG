Luke Skywalker is a Jedi Knight and the son of Anakin Skywalker, who became Darth Vader.
Darth Vader, originally Anakin Skywalker, is a central character in the Star Wars universe.
Princess Leia is Luke Skywalker's twin sister and a leader in the Rebel Alliance.
Han Solo is a skilled pilot and smuggler who becomes a general in the Rebel Alliance.
Yoda is a wise and powerful Jedi Master who trains Luke Skywalker.
Obi-Wan Kenobi is a Jedi Master who mentors both Anakin and Luke Skywalker.
The Death Star is a massive space station capable of destroying entire planets.
The Force is a mystical energy that gives Jedi and Sith their powers.
The Rebel Alliance fights against the Galactic Empire to restore freedom to the galaxy.
Emperor Palpatine is the Sith Lord who rules the Galactic Empire.
Chewbacca is a Wookiee warrior and Han Solo's loyal friend and co-pilot.
R2-D2 is an astromech droid known for his resourcefulness and bravery.
C-3PO is a protocol droid fluent in over six million forms of communication.
Lando Calrissian is a suave businessman and gambler who aids the Rebel Alliance.
Boba Fett is a notorious bounty hunter hired by Darth Vader.
